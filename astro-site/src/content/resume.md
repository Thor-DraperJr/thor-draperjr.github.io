## Professional Experience

### Sr. Security Solution Engineer
**Microsoft Corporation** | *April 2024 - Present*

I own the full security stack for the Modern Workplace suite across U.S. Healthcare payors, providers, and Life Sciences organizations. My work covers protection, detection, and remediation of security vulnerabilities alongside data retention and loss prevention. I provide pre-sales technical support to help customers and partners architect, deploy, and operate Microsoft identity and security solutions.

### Azure Network Support Engineer, ARR
**Microsoft Corporation** | *April 2021 - March 2024*

On the Rapid Response team, I supported and managed 18 IaaS technologies in Azure, focusing on critical severity situations and service request backlogs. As ARR Team Lead, I coordinated cross-team efforts to proactively review Azure architecture and security posture. I guided clients through Azure deployments, ensuring they got the most out of tools like Microsoft Sentinel and Log Analytics.

Outside my core role, I organized mentoring events for students and hosted educational events at the Charlotte campus as the ISSA education director.

### Cyber Security Instructor
**Trilogy Education** | *January 2020 - March 2024*

I taught Windows/Linux administration, ethical hacking, and SIEM management to career-changers entering cybersecurity. I built hands-on labs and cert prep curricula that equipped students for real-world challenges. Earned the highest Net-promoter score in 2020. The teaching sharpened my consulting work at Microsoft by forcing me to explain complex concepts clearly.

### Information Systems Engineer
**Biz Technology Solutions** | *November 2020 - May 2021*

I led client communications for on-premises to cloud migrations across healthcare, real estate, and other sectors. Used PowerShell to identify synchronization issues and corrupted profiles. Conducted initial network audits as a sales engineer, identifying technology gaps and securing follow-up appointments. Handled tier-two client escalations on a daily basis.

### Network Administrator
**Nucentric Solution** | *January 2020 - December 2020*

I held multiple positions and earned three promotions during my time here. Built ESXi and Hyper-V environments, developed PowerShell scripts and modules to automate administrative tasks, and used a home lab to simulate production scenarios while pursuing certifications.

### Technical Account Manager (NC/SC/VA)
**G-Net Solutions Inc.** | *July 2019 - January 2020*

I sold and supported Aternity from Riverbed as a value-added reseller. Identified and closed business opportunities while providing technical guidance on deployment and integration best practices for client environments.
